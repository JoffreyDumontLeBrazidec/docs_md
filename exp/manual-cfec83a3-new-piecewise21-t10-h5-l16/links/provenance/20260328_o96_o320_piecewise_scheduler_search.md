## Search Definition
- Phase 1 stays in the existing `experimental_piecewise` scheduler family.
- Stage 0 screens the high segment with `sigma_transition=10`, `sigma_max in {1000, 10000, 100000}`, and `num_steps_high in {3, 5, 7}` while holding the low segment at `karras`, `num_steps_low=20`.
- Stage 1 fixes `sigma_max=1000` and sweeps the agreed exact grid `sigma_transition in {5, 7, 10}`, `num_steps_high in {5, 7, 9}`, and `num_steps_low in {13, 16, 19}` with no extra transition controls and no duplicated stage-0 control rows.
- Stage 2 refreshes the coupling around the provisional winner by retesting the local neighborhood in `sigma_max` and `num_steps_high`.
- The proxy-first gate is canonical. Only top finalists move to full `Aug 26-30`.

## Acceptance Rule
- Spectra guardrail comes first.
- A candidate must stay within `0.003` of the checkpoint's current `piecewise30` spectra mean score and must not lose more than `0.01` on any one of `10u`, `10v`, or `2t`.
- Among guardrail passes, rank by mean Idalia TC delta, then worst-checkpoint Idalia TC delta, then fewer total steps.
- Do not replace the current default unless a finalist is non-negative on both checkpoints after the full-contract pass.
